*   **Student Ledger Access from Lessons**: Provide an option on lesson blocks to view and make real-time changes to a student's financial ledger, ideally as a non-blocking, floating interface.
*   **Enhanced Enrollment Financials**: Incorporate  into enrollments, allow payments to be allocated towards a grand total for an enrollment, and display student names and financial summaries on the main Enrollments page.

*   **Lesson History & Navigation**: Provide a view of a student's complete lesson history within the ledger, with the ability to mark past lessons as attended and click on any lesson to navigate the calendar to its specific date.
*   **Real-time Synchronization**: Ensure all new enrollments, payments, and lesson history updates are automatically reflected in the corresponding student ledger and other related components, maintaining accurate, real-time records for reporting and daily operations.
